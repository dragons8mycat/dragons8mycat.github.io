Welcome

Hi, I'm Nick Duggan — a Chartered Geographer and Fellow of the Royal Geographical Society with over two decades working at the forefront of the geospatial industry. I'm currently Research & Innovation Manager at Idox Geospatial, where I get to explore the intersection of geospatial data, artificial intelligence, and strategic innovation — helping to shape new products and research across land, planning, and renewables.

I was fortunate to be recognised in the GEO100 as one of the world's most influential geospatial professionals, and I'm passionate about giving back to the community. Whether it's speaking at conferences, writing about the future of location intelligence and GeoAI, or mentoring the next generation of geospatial talent, I love sharing what I've learned along the way.

Have a look around — below you'll find a selection of my work, from 3D city models and tilt-shift cartography to bathymetric visualisations and beyond. I hope you enjoy it.
